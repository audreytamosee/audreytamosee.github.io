DISNEYLAND ENTERTAINMENT TOP ATTRACTION

Toy Soldier Parachute Drop

Drop from the sky with Toy Soldiers from Toy Story during a parachuting drill with thrills!

Age Interest: Kids, Tweens, Teens, Adults, Single Rider Available

Orbitron

Location: Tomorrowland

Guests Can Be: Any Height

Age Interest: All Ages

Thrill Type: Small Drops, Slow Rides, Spinning

Location: Adventureland

Guests Can Be: Any Height

Location: Toystory Land

Guests Can Be: 120cm (40in) or Taller

Thrill Type: Big Drops, Thrill Rides

Location: Grizzly Gulch

Age Interest: Tweens, Teens, Adults

Cinderella Carousel

Spin around, up and down to joyous music atop a regal steed at this classic attraction inspired by Disney’s Cinderella.

Location: Fantasyland

Guests Can Be: Any Height

Age Interest: All Ages

Thrill Type: Slow Rides, Spinning
