About Jungle River Cruise:

The Jungle Cruise is a river boat attraction located in Adventureland at many Disney Parks worldwide, namely Disneyland, Magic Kingdom, Tokyo Disneyland, and Hongkong Disneyland (the attraction at Hong Kong Disneyland named "Jungle River Cruise"). This attraction is the longest queue at Disneyland, because in this attraction you can explore a jungle with a storyteller and there are many attractive things like an elephant can spray a water from their mouth

Location: Main Street, U.S.A

About Flights of Fantasy Parade:

You should watch this parade! The music is so darling! The song is in English and Chinese! It is super catchy and fun to listen to, also interactive – the parade makes frequent stops in the route where they hand out ribbon wands to kids in the audience to come dance with them! It goes on for an entire song so everyone really gets into it! If you can time it just right to be by the start of the parade with the Mickey and Minnie float, its a great place for this. If you watch the parade in front of the Castle or at the beginning of Main Street you can catch the stops.

Start Price: $1350HKD - $5850HKD/night
